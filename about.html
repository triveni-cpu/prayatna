Group examinations


Group examinations in India are competitive recruitment exams conducted by govt bodies to select candidates for various govt posts.
The term "GROUP" refers to the classification of posts based on their level of responsibility, authority, pay scale and role within the govt.
These are broadly divided into 2 levels. They are:

1.State-Level group Examinations
2. Central-Level group Examinations



State-Level Group Examinations

The state-level group examinations are conducted by the State Public Service Commission (SPSC) to recruit candidates for state govt jobs. These are generally classified as:

    Group 1 -
    Highest-level executive / administrative posts.
    Example: Deputy Collector, DSP, CTO, RDO, etc..,
    Group 2 -
    Executive / subordinate posts below Group 1.
    Example: Municipal Commissioner, Sub-Registor, Asst.Commercial Tax Officer(ACTO)etc..,
    Group 3 -
    clerical/ non-executive posts.
    Example: panchayat Secretary, junior Employment Officer, Auditor etc..,
    Group 4 -
    Lower division clerks / Assistants
    Example: junior Assistant, Typist, Record Assistant, etc..,

Conducted By:


  • APPSC-Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission
  • TPPSC -Telangana Public Service Commission
  • KPSC -Karnataka Public Service Commission
  • MPPSC -Mizoram Public Service Commission
  • APSC -Assam Public Service Commission
  • Goa PSC -Goa Public Service Commission
  • TNPSC -Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission
  • APPSC -Arunachal Pradesh Public Service Commission etc..,
Central-Level Group Examinations

The central-level group examinations are competitive exams conducted by the Central Government of India to recruit candidates into various Group A, Group B, Grooup C, and Group D posts in central govt departments, ministries, and organizations. These are generally classified as:

    Group A -
    Highest-level officers.
    Example: IRSE, IDSE,CSE etc..,(via Engineering Services Examination), IFoS (via Indian Forest Service), IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS etc...(via UPSC) etc..,
    Group B -
    Mid-level executive / non-gazetted posts
    Example: DANICS, DANIPS, PCS, PPS (via UPSC), ASO, non-gazetted inspectors,JE etc.,(via SSC) DEPR/DSIM officers etc., (via RBI) etc..,
    Group C -
    clerical/ non-executive / support staff posts.
    Example: Tax Assistant (CBDT & CBIC), UDC, CAG, CGA, peon, daftary, junior Gestetner Operator etc.., (via SSC), BANK Clerks (via IBPS & bANKS)etc..,
    Group D -
    Manual & Non-clerical staff
    Now largely Merged with Group C.

Conducted By:


  • UPSC-Union Public Service Commission
  • SSC -Staff Selection Commission
  • RRB -Railway Recruitment Board
  • IBPS -Institute of Banking Personnel Selection
  • FSSAI -Food Safety and Standards Authority of India
  • ISRO-Indian Space Research Organization
  • DRDO -Defence Research & Development Organization
  • NTA -National Testing Agency etc..,
